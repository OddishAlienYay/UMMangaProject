The UM Manga Project

Welcome to the website of the UM Manga Project, a joke manga about a zombie apocalypse that happens, centered around kids from University Middle, Memphis TN.


We are currently working on the teaser for #1. There is no ETA at this time, it could be released today, or in a year. Who knows?



Artists: Everett Graves (basic design and panel sketches), Noah Lara(final touches, inking, finished art and finished panels);


Editors: Korbin Bilbrey, Trent Reed, Jax Fontanilla
